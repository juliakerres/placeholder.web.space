how to pack a meal? Why do containers mimic the shape of food? Or does the content adapt to the outer form? Through the binding of this booklet, I explore these questions in an ironic way. Hidden inside the folded pages, the shape of the food matches the shape of the container. An overview of the food containers can be found on the unfolded back of the book cover.

soft extensions. in this rigid world where lovelessness has become part of our system, where we see that our society and its structures only function through power and domination. In a world where everything we learn and know is based on and shaped by domination, capitalism and patriarchy, as bell hooks describes in "all about love" (link). I want to imagine a world where softness becomes the measure of all things! Representative of this thought, my soft extension becomes a coping tool that you can sew into your sleeve and carry with you whenever you feel the need for softness.
                                                                    
touchy type. typography in use and in development to deformation. It comes in sharp edges and light smell. Wash for 20-30 seconds, rinse thoroughly and dry your hands afterwards. Inspired by a music booklet of melodies from famous soaps, the soaps and matching soundwave towels were created.

                                
open source memory. people develop methods and tools to pass on knowledge and experience to the next generations. How does this idea develop in a highly technological future? What would a world look like in which private thoughts no longer exist? The imaginary application gives a glimpse into this fictional reality and the human mind, presented through a social platform where memories can be shared online.

german souvenir shop. a soft approximation to the ordinary German culture through the perspective of a German. After a visual investigation of German culture (link)it‘s aesthetics was made accessible through souvenirs: a range of napkins with meat prints – postcards of special parts form the german Autobahn – tablecloth fixations in the shape of pickles and hardboiled eggs.

                    
soft encyclopedia. soft encyclopedia explores the web’s intrinsic network nature as a narrative tool. By claiming hypertext’s non-sequential and non-linear structures as an antidote to an alienating, heroic, and causal mode of reasoning dominating Western perception and belief system, soft encyclopedia enables connections between links that initially seemed distant. It fosters associations and opens up new perspectives, constantly (re)linking and (re)shaping truths. As a different kind of narrative, as a container “[...] holding things in a particular, powerful relation to one another and to us.”(link)Conceived as the dawn of a new world order by its creators, the web arises from the same systematic essence inherent to the fabric of existence—as a network of connections and interdependencies. These thoughts are made tangible through an installation centered around a hypertext document, where words evolve into a poem, a story, a lore, a spell, a ritual. Composed by the user’s navigation between hyperlinks, the document’s URL becomes the carrier of the narrative. Read my ba thesishere.
